About Us

Everest Aero Solutions (EAS) is a leading independent specialist in the supply and repair management of certified aircraft spare parts, with a wide presence in the United States, the Caribbean, Africa, Middle-East, India & Nepal. Everest Aero Solutions is a FAA AC 0056B and TAC2000 compliant company based in Kathmandu, Nepal. The Company was established in 2016, by a team of experts in aviation, overhaul and marketing. By establishing mutually beneficial trusted and established partnerships to transform operational efficiency, EAS utilises its trusted and established vendor supply chain relationships to assist in cost savings and increasing efficiency for customers. EAS is highly regarded for its 24/7/365 and critical response service excellence and has a dedicated team of sales and service, located worldwide, along with global stock locations. Our sister company, United Aero Solutions, is situated in Florida, United States. However, we have partnered with suppliers from around the world to support our customers for spare needs on their regional, narrow-body and wide-body aircraft.

Vision

To establish as a leading aircraft teardown and spare parts provider with an intention to provide full support to our Operator Clients.

Mission

To grow into an efficient and customer-oriented aviation parts provider, worldwide.
